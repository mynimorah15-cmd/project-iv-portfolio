Cookout

2025, 20x20in, oil on canvas

This is a loose, abstract-impressionistic take on the feeling of the traditional southern black-American cookout. Namyni explores the feeling of family, community, culture, and grief in this piece. The dreamy, pinks and greens are meant to evoke a state of nostalgia; the viewer should reminiss of the closeness of family in childhood. Regardless of cultural background.
