Say It Loud!

2025, 24 x 30in., oil on canvas

This painting is highlighting the resilience, artistry and authenticity of black people throughout history. The 70s was one of the most culturally innovative times for America and we have black people to thank for that. (Hip hop, disco, rap, fashion etc. ) Despite the active laws against them, they persevered through dance, music, and big ass Afros. A time where being black as hell was cool.
