Self Portrait

2025, 24 x 36, oil on canvas

Red is Namyni's favorite color and so, it seemed reasonable to paint herself in this warm light despite the coldness of the interior space. Her personality is warm but its often dulled by perpetual thought. She was loosely thinking of this space as her mind. Namyni finds herself alone within her thoughts more often than not, so this space was fitting. Just wondering with her headphones on.
