Stankface

2025, 11x18in, oil on canvas

This is a loose study expanding on black subjectivity and its relation to music . Music is a huge part of black everyday life and Namyni wants to represent the different spaces that generates. Black music and dance is one of the most important contributions to pop culture. Even before black black-American people were allowed to showcase the artform themselves. She wanted to make sure viewers acknowledge and appreciate the origins.
